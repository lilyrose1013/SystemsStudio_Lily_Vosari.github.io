Project 1 Self as Systems

This is the Project 1:
Self as System:

My ideation behind this project was based on the emotional connection between music and my emotions
after downloading my data from Spotify and simplifying the data into a 2 day period. I wanted to represent my emotional state through colors and shapes
that would represent my most listened to songs and artists thought shapes, with the larger shapes typically representing my most listened to songs.
I wanted to use color to help in the representation with colors relaying the mood of the songs along with the typical color representations displayed within
modern media.
