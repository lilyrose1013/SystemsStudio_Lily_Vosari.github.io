Draft 2 — Rain

In my 3 in 3 project, I wanted to create a emotional represnetaion of my music data and how with the 2 days of data collected it relects my emotional state in time.
What I was able to conclude from the data that I collected was that I was going the a sad emotional state and my music was able to relfect that with the songs chosen and the number of times
they where played.

I chose a blue color scale to represent the Sadness that was communicated thoughout the songs I used raindrops and the sizes of them to represent the morst listened to songs; as the songs
become more listened to the rain drops become bigger and darker in color, in addition I plan to use javascript to communicate the concept of tears and raindrops falling
to communicate that relationship between emotional state and the data collected from my Spotify.

Meedium: Javascript, HTML, CSS
